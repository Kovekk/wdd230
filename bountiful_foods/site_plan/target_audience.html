Target Audience

The target audience are people looking to make a healthy change. Prospective site visiters will most likely be from the ages of 25 to 40. They will be people looking to improve their habits. They will be in stable working positions that make enough money to want more convenience. Expected job positions of visiters will be young CEO's, managers, and tech jobs. Most visiters will know how to use the internet very well whether through social media usage or from work.

Persona 1
Name Craig Johanson
Job Title Team Manager
Demographics 35 years old, Married, Has 2 Kids, Masters in Managerial Work
Goals and Tasks He is a hard working leader who spends as much time at work as he does with his family. At home he strives to be an example to his young kids, especially in healthy eating. He is always looking for ways to improve the lives of his young children, especially if it is something the kids will enjoy.
Environment Spends a lot of time at a computer especially at work. He is an expert internet user who does research for work often. He uses internet communication software regularly and is on the web for meetings or work business.
