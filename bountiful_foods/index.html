La Jolla

Come visit our food truck at 3595 John Hopkins court in San Diego for fresh organic juice! Sip on our delicious juice as you walk along the shores with world-famous views in La Jolla. La Jolla, also known as "the jewel" of San Deigo, hosts outdoor activities such as hiking, hang gliding, kayaking, and more. Whales can also be seen migrating to and from Baja California. Or, if you prefer indoor activities, visit La Jolla's aquarium or museum of contemporary art.
