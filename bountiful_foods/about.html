Our Mission

Our mission at Bountiful Foods is to support local farmers and provide locals and tourists with convenient access to healthy organic fruit and produce. Some produce is sourced from (inter)national suppliers, but most of our products are directly sourced from local farmers. We provide convenient access to healthy organic food by taking walk-ins at our storefront and food trucks, where customers can purchase fresh, made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags. We also proide order options both via phone and our website.

History

The storefront of Bountiful Foods first opened on August 27, 2018, located in Carlsbad, CA at (ADDRESS). Since then, we have added a dozen food trucks, which operate at various loctions along the coastal region from LA to San Diego.The idea for Bountiful Foods began when our owner, Paige Turner, noticed that access to healthy organic produce required frequenting farmers markets and grocery stores. There was a lack of convenient options for locals and tourists to gain access to fresh organic fruit on-the-go along the coastal region. Turner decided to solve this problem and follow her passion for increasing access to healthy organic produce, creating Bountiful Foods.
