Site purpose

This site is being designed for the purpose of bringing together local businesses in Provo and promoting the business of the members. The Home and Discover pages will be designed to attract people to the site. The discover page will specifically provide historical information and demographic data. A directory page will provide a list of information regarding the businesses that are a part of the chamber. Businesses wanting to join can do so through the join page. The join page will have four levels of membership options each with different perks. There will also be a contact page will have the information needed to contact the chamber officers and board of directors.
