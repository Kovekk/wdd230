Target audience

The target audience for this site has two different parts that are described in the site purpose. The first half of the target audience is the business owners of provo. One part of the sites purpose is to bring together the local businesses of provo. This requires that business owners come to the site and join the chamber of commerce. The second half of the target audience is the people of provo and any visiters/tourists that could use the services of the businesses that become members of the chamber. This second half of the audience will be accessing the site either for historical information through our discover page or to find businesses to support or visit through our discovery page. The end goal is to have people find local businesses and use their services.
